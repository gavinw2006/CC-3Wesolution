FAQ

Common Questions

How quickly can you start on a new project? ▾
For most services we can begin within 3–5 business days of scoping. Urgent Digital Risk Protection incidents can be activated same day.
Do you work with clients outside Asia-Pacific? ▾
Yes. While our roots are in Asia-Pacific, we actively serve clients in Australia, Europe, and North America. All our services are delivered remotely with timezone-friendly communication.
What makes your Digital Risk Protection different? ▾
We combine 15 years of frontline DRP experience with the AI-driven detection platform from our partner Cyberoo.ai — a partnership now entering its 5th year. Most agencies offer expertise or technology, not both.
Can you handle just one service, or do I need a bundle? ▾
Absolutely — each service is available standalone. Many clients start with web design or hosting and add DRP or domain management as their needs grow. There's no obligation to bundle.
Do you offer ongoing retainer support? ▾
Yes. Most of our clients work with us on a monthly retainer basis, which includes proactive monitoring, maintenance, reporting, and dedicated account support. We'll recommend the right model during your free consultation.
