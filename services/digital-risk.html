Phishing & Fake Sites

Continuous scanning detects fraudulent websites impersonating your brand in real time — then we take them down fast, minimizing customer exposure.

📱

Fake App Detection

Rogue mobile applications in the App Store and Google Play stealing your brand equity are identified, reported, and removed before widespread damage occurs.

Our Methodology

Detect. Analyze. Disrupt. Repeat.

Our three-phase intelligence cycle runs continuously — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, across every digital channel.

1

Detection & Signal Collection

AI engines sweep domains, ad networks, app stores, social platforms, and consumer report streams — aggregating threat signals into a unified risk feed in real time.

2

Intelligence & Correlation

Signals are correlated into campaign-level intelligence. Attackers, infrastructure patterns, victim targeting profiles, and risk scores are mapped for your brand, channel, and region.

3

Disruption & Takedown

Validated threats are converted into takedown requests submitted to registrars, hosting providers, ad platforms, and app stores — with expert follow-through until resolved.

4

Reporting & Continuous Defense

Detailed incident reports, compliance evidence packs, and trend analysis are delivered to your team — while the cycle continues, adapting to emerging attacker tactics.
