Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Which package is right for my business?

AI Starter is ideal for businesses deploying their first AI solution — a focused proof of concept that delivers quick wins. AI Business suits companies ready to transform multiple functions and build lasting AI capability. AI Enterprise is for organizations with complex, multi-system environments needing a dedicated AI team.

Can I upgrade between packages?

Yes. Most clients start with AI Starter or AI Business and upgrade as they see results. Everything built in an earlier package is preserved and extended — you never have to rebuild from scratch.

What systems can you integrate with?

We integrate with all major CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot, Microsoft Dynamics), ERP (SAP, Oracle, NetSuite), helpdesk (Zendesk, Freshdesk, Intercom), and communication platforms (Slack, Microsoft Teams, WhatsApp). If it has an API, we can connect to it.

How is my data protected?

We design every integration with data privacy as a first principle — encrypting data in transit and at rest, enforcing least-privilege access controls, and using enterprise-grade LLM providers that do not train on customer data by default. Enterprise package clients can opt for fully on-premises deployment.

What if the AI doesn't perform as expected?

Every package includes a defined evaluation period with agreed success metrics. If performance falls short, we iterate at no additional cost until we meet the agreed targets. Our business depends on your results — we are invested in making this work.
