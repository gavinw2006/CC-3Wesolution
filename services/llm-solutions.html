Our Integration Process

From Idea to Production Integration

A proven four-stage process that moves quickly from problem definition to a live, measurable deployment — with your team involved at every milestone.

1

Discovery & Use Case Selection

We assess your current systems, data landscape, and business goals — then identify the highest-ROI LLM integration opportunities and prioritize them by impact and feasibility.

2

Model Selection & Architecture

We select the optimal LLM provider and deployment architecture — cloud API, private cloud, or on-premises — and design the integration layer to connect with your existing systems securely.

3

Fine-Tuning & RAG Implementation

We configure retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) pipelines and, where appropriate, fine-tune models on your proprietary data — ensuring the LLM speaks your domain language with authority.

4

Deployment, Monitoring & Optimization

We deploy into production with comprehensive logging, quality monitoring, cost controls, and model performance dashboards — then optimize continuously based on real-world usage patterns.
